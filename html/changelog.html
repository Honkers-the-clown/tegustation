10 February 2021

Maurukas updated:

  • A Public mining shuttle dock has been installed near the holodeck on Kilostation
  • Kilostation's incinerator has been expanded to accommodate the HFR and provide Atmospherics with an additional work area
  • Adds a trash chute to tool storage and the morgue on Kilostation
  • Removed the nonfunctional public mining dock from Kilostation aux base construction
  • A number of small piping issues on Kilostation have been fixed

nightred updated:

  • Icebox: Genetics monkey pen is larger
  • Icebox: Toxins area reworked layout
  • Icebox: Toxins burn chamber moved to prevent overheating Science hallway

04 February 2021

  • Repeated EMPs should no longer render a secborg's disabler permanently unusable.

Fikou updated:

  • adds vars for damage done by temp on simplemobs

Gamer025 updated:

  • The recycler now fully recycles items, including their contents

Kylerace updated:

  • its now slightly harder to abuse the holodeck to break the second law of thermodynamics

LemonInTheDark updated:

  • Fixes spacevines seemingly randomly dying
  • Config works again, WAIT I JUST GAVE ALL THE POWER BACK TO THE ADMIN TEA-

Low3 updated:

  • Admin Log and Show Admins in Secrets panel now render unicode characters.

Melbert updated:

  • Fixed some incorrect args for visible messages in piggybacking and bone-setting.

NotRanged updated:

  • Emitters are now hitscan beams.
  • Fixed a bug with hitscan beams and reflector boxes.

necromanceranne updated:

  • Turns the Duffel Bag Curse into a melee spell, with significant rebalances and changes to rework the spell for being a melee spell.
  • Dealing with the duffel bag is slightly more easy, but it inflicts you with a lot of fairly disruptive debuffs while attached, and not dealing with the bag will hurt quite a bit.
  • Tidies up some of the living duffel bag code.
  • Replaces improvised shotguns with Pipeguns (with a special variant for those willing to go through arbitrary bullshit to acquire it and sheer good luck).
  • Now all guns can be set to misfire and swap ammunition.
  • Misfire chance is incremental as you fire the weapon, and not a flat static chance. This can be reset by using a piece of cloth on the gun and 10 seconds of cleaning.
  • This has been applied to the detective revolver, but it only increments while using .357 bullets.
  • Guns can have damage multipliers attached for the bullets they fire. The pipegun (but not the regal version) is the first example with a 75% damage output.
  • The Ice Hermit now has a regal pipegun instead of a Mosin Nagant.
  • The bandolier fits 7.62mm.
  • You can construct receivers and rifle stocks in the crafting menu.
  • Detaches the magical rifles from the boltaction subtype, since they are just basically not using any variables attached to that subtype and made from a series of early returns.
  • Changes the carrying traits on Latex/Nitrile gloves into a skillchip.
  • Medical doctors start the round with a quick carry skillchip (was the latex glove trait).
  • Paramedics and the CMO start the round with a quicker carry skillchip (was the nitrile glove trait).
